HEART DISEASE PREDICTION

  • Python,Flask,javascript,HTML

We have developed a web application focused on predicting heart disease specifically for diabetic individuals using advanced machine learning techniques such as K-Nearest Neighbor (KNN), Decision Trees, Logistic Regression, and Non-linear models. By analyzing data from Heartwave tests, our goal is to significantly reduce unexpected heart attack deaths among diabetics. This initiative addresses the critical issue of symptomless heart attacks, particularly alarming for diabetics. Unlike many existing systems, our approach targets diabetes-related heart conditions with exceptional accuracy, benefiting at-risk younger generations and advancing predictive health technology.
